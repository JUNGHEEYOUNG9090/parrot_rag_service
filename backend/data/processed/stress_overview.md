# Stress in Parrots (스트레스 개요)

## 1. Definition (정의)

Stress in parrots refers to a physiological and behavioral response to environmental, social, or health-related pressure that disrupts normal behavior, feeding, sleep, or interaction patterns.

앵무새의 스트레스(stress)는 환경적 변화, 사회적 요인, 건강 문제로 인해 정상적인 행동, 식사, 수면, 사회적 상호작용이 방해받는 상태를 의미합니다.

---

## 2. Key Stress Signals (스트레스 주요 신호 / Signs)

Common stress signs in parrots include:

- Feather plucking (깃털 뽑기)
- Aggression or biting (공격성, 물기)
- Freezing or immobility (움직임 감소, 얼어붙음)
- Excessive vocalization or screaming (과도한 울음)
- Loss of appetite (식욕 감소)
- Restlessness or pacing (불안한 움직임)
- Sleep disruption (수면 장애)

## 3. Causes of Stress (스트레스 원인)

Stress can be caused by multiple factors:

### Environmental causes

- cage_placement.md (케이지 위치 문제)

- moving_home.md (이사 및 환경 변화)

### Social causes

- lack of interaction

### Health-related causes

- illness or pain
- feather disorders

## 5. Stress vs Normal Behavior (중요 구분)

Not all behaviors are abnormal.

- Chewing objects = normal enrichment OR stress indicator
- Vocalization = communication OR stress signal
- Feather preening = normal grooming OR stress symptom

Context is required.

## 7. When Stress Becomes a Health Issue

Stress can lead to:

- feather plucking disorders
- immune system weakening
- digestive issues
- chronic behavioral problems
